Meet your trainer!

Hi! I’m Lindsey Rahn, originally from Washington State, now located in San Antonio, TX. I’m a wife and mother of 3 noisy boys. As a life-long fitness enthusiast, I’ve run several marathons and half marathons, and competed in power lifting. During covid, I earned my NASM certifications for both personal training and nutrition and started Lindsey Rahn Fitness. I’m passionate about helping clients overcome their health obstacles and empowering them to take control of their wellness. When I’m not working, you can find me playing the drum set and piano, launching rockets at the park, and losing at Monopoly.
