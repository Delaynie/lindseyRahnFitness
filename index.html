Nutrition Sessions

As a certified nutritionist, I'll work with you to build a nutrition plan that's right for your body, your goals, and your tastes. In a world where you're constantly bombarded with the next fad diet, the next new pill, and society's obsession with being the "right" weight, I'll help you sort through the noise to make a lifestyle that's right for you. We'll focus on healthy habits and proper portioning for long term health and short term success without starving yourself or cutting out the foods you love.


  • → One-on-one sessions conducted live over Zoom.
  • → Meet weekly at a set schedule, or meet a la carte on your own schedule, 30 or 60 minute sessions.
  • → Personalised nutrition plan for your body, activity level, goals and tastes.
  • → Coaching will include habit formation, accountability, appropriate portioning, and nutrition education to help you be successful long term.
  • → Included in my Exclusive Members Only Community where you'll receive helpful content about motivation, exercise, nutrition, and support from community members to help you be more successful.

Exercise Plans

Everyone is different, and everyone likes to move their body in a different way. As a certified personal trainer, I will build you the best workout for your unique lifestyle. Every workout is made individually, with the client’s needs in mind.


  • → 5 week plan using Progressive Overload to improve fitness quickly.
  • → Contact-free. Program delivered to your inbox.
  • → Workout on your own schedule, no live meetings.
  • → For gym or for home, your program will use the equipment you have available.
  • → Built for your unique goals, preferences, and lifestyle
  • → For any fitness level, whether you’re new to exercise or an old pro.
  • → Complete workouts or supplemental programs to support your sport.
  • → Send me messages or videos any time you need help.
  • → Included in my Exclusive Members Only Community where you'll receive helpful content about motivation, exercise, and nutrition, and you can participate in accountability checks.

Live Weekly Accountability Groups

One of the best predictors of success is if you’re willing to reach out and ask for help. In a live accountability group, you’ll have a chance to give and receive support in a social setting.


  • → Contact-free Zoom groups.
  • → Live coaching and accountability from me.
  • → A safe and inclusive space to share your struggles and get feedback.
  • → You’ll have the same members in your group every week so you can make friends.
  • → Small groups, up to 12, for support and community
  • → Included in my Exclusive Members Only Community where you'll receive helpful content about motivation, exercise, and nutrition, and you can participate in accountability checks.

Meet your trainer!

Hi! I’m Lindsey Rahn, originally from Washington State, now located in San Antonio, TX. I’m a wife and mother of 3 noisy boys. As a life-long fitness enthusiast, I’ve run several marathons and half marathons, and competed in power lifting. During covid, I earned my NASM certifications for both personal training and nutrition and started Lindsey Rahn Fitness. I’m passionate about helping clients overcome their health obstacles and empowering them to take control of their wellness. When I’m not working, you can find me playing the drum set and piano, launching rockets at the park, and losing at Monopoly.
